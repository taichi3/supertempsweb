Get to know about Supertemps

Supertemps International Ltd is a leading company in Commercial Services, Accredited Training Services, and Consultancy Services. We take pride in our ability to create space in the provision of Apprenticeship, Industrial Attachment, and Indentured Learnership through steadfast commitment to innovation, sustainability, and superior services.

By offering personalized solutions designed to meet the unique needs of our clients both locally and internationally, we ensure our services remain impactful and relevant. Our product range continues to grow through constant investment, driven by active customer feedback and our dedication to creating real-time, practical solutions.

We aspire to fulfill all requests and create the best bespoke systems specifically tailored to each company’s needs. With years of experience in the industry, Supertemps has established itself as a reliable, committed, and trusted partner delivering quality products and services.

Our facilitators are seasoned industry experts with years of practice and experience, working as professionals and trainers in their respective fields, capable of handling projects of any scale and complexity with excellence.
